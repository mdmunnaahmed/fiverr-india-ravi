Question: 01
Q1. Consider the following statements:
  • Procurator System is the citizen’s grievance redressal in Socialist countries.
  • Administrative System of Courts is the citizen’s grievance redressal in Common Wealth countries.
  • Ombudsman System is the citizen’s grievance redressal in African countries.
Answer - B
  • The earliest democratic institution created in the world for the redressal of citizens’ grievance is the Scandinavian institution of Ombudsman.
  • The institution of Ombudsman was first created in Sweden in 1809. ‘Ombud’ is a Swedish term and refers to a person who acts as the representative or spokesman of another person.
  • Administrative System of Courts is in African countries, and Ombudman in Common Wealth and Scandinavian Countries.
